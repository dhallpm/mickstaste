# Micks Framework Source Update

Last updated: 2026-06-25

Doc's Sports free public pages are now part of the Micks framework research layer.

For each review, check at least these three pages:

- https://www.docsports.com/free-picks/
- https://www.docsports.com/free-picks/?start=20
- https://www.docsports.com/free-picks/?start=40

Use the articles as research inputs only. Extract article angle, line or price, reasoning, injury notes, matchup notes, trends, and any agreement across writers.

Do not copy any selection blindly. Compare every article to current odds, market movement, injuries, implied probability, best-number discipline, and Micks grading before it affects confidence.

Premium or paywalled content is excluded.
