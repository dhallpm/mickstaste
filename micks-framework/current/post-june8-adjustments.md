# Micks Picks Post-June 8 Adjustments

## Reason For Adjustment
June 8 showed a clear framework leak:

- WNBA official plays went 2-0.
- Pick of the Day won.
- Role-stable NBA prop logic was useful.
- Full-game MLB totals were the leak.
- Brewers/Athletics Under 11 should have been capped harder because of Las Vegas Ballpark, heat/altitude, and high-total volatility.
- Yankees/Guardians Under 8 was a reasonable handicap but too fragile for parlay use.

## New Rule
Do not let projection edge alone push volatile MLB totals onto the official card.

A full-game MLB total must be risk-adjusted for:

- Park.
- Weather.
- Altitude.
- Umpire.
- Starter length.
- Bullpen quality/rest.
- Lineup power/contact.
- Market movement.

## MLB Totals Adjustment
Any MLB total in an extreme run environment is capped at **B-/Watchlist** unless at least 4 run-suppression confirmations exist.

Extreme run environment includes:

- Las Vegas Ballpark.
- Minor-league or neutral-site hitter park.
- Heat or altitude boosting carry.
- Total of 10.5 or higher.
- Homer-prone starter.
- Tired or bad bullpen.
- Market moving Over.

## Full-Game Under Adjustment
If Under projection edge exists but park/weather favors offense:

- Downgrade one full grade, or
- Move to watchlist, or
- Switch to first-5 if starter path is the real edge.

## Parlay Adjustment
No parlay leg may use a fragile high-volatility MLB total.

If a total has blow-up risk, it cannot be used in the main parlay.

## WNBA Upgrade Adjustment
WNBA model-gap spreads can be upgraded more aggressively.

- 4+ point model gap plus injury/rotation confirmation = B+ eligible.
- 5+ point model gap plus market support = A-candidate eligible.

## Props Adjustment
Role-stable props and lineup-confirmed MLB hitter props can outrank volatile MLB game totals.

Prioritize:

- Confirmed lineup/role.
- Plus-money price.
- Weak opponent matchup.
- Independent projection edge.
- Low dependency on one fragile game script.

## New Tags
Add these tags to future analysis:

- Extreme Run Environment
- Volatile Total Cap
- Park/Weather Conflict
- Role-Stable Prop
- A-Hunt Candidate
- B+ Near-A

## Required Output Changes
Every official MLB total must include:

- Park/Weather Risk.
- Blow-Up Risk: Low / Medium / High.
- Whether volatility capped the grade.

Every A/B+ candidate must include:

- A Grade Gate Result.
- Evidence count.
- Market misprice reason.
- Unresolved conflict, if any.

## Bottom Line
The system should not become looser. It should become sharper.

More A grades should come from deeper market search: props, first-5, team totals, stale lines, and injury/lineup edges, not from upgrading normal B plays.
