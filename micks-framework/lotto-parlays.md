# Micks Picks Lotto Parlays Framework

## Purpose
- Lotto Parlays are high-variance small-unit tickets, not core bankroll plays.
- 5-leg, 6-leg, 7-leg, and 8-leg safe lotto parlays route to Lotto Parlays.
- Parlays, SGPs, lotto props, and round robins must not route to Props Lab.

## Construction Rules
- Use only legs with confirmed market prices and current availability.
- Avoid stacking too many legs that fail from the same hidden assumption unless the ticket is intentionally correlated and labeled that way.
- Prefer legs with independent edges or clean positive correlation.
- Do not include stale lines, unclear injury spots, or players with uncertain minutes.
- Keep longshot or futures-style tickets in Longshots unless they are explicitly safe lotto parlay builds.

## Safe Version Vs Value Version
- Safe version: lower-volatility leg mix, fewer fragile player props, clearer injury/news context.
- Value version: higher payout expression with stricter unit size and cutoff discipline.
- Publish the safe version when the ticket is meant for the public card.
- Keep value versions in review unless every leg has source verification.

## Leg Count Guidance
- 5-leg: preferred safe lotto size when each leg has a clear role and playable number.
- 6-leg: acceptable when one or more legs are low-volatility or strongly correlated.
- 7-leg: review carefully for duplicate risk and hidden correlation.
- 8-leg: smallest units only and requires the strongest source verification.

## Units And Risk
- Lotto parlays are small-unit plays by default.
- Do not size a lotto ticket like a core A/B/C pick.
- Any ticket with uncertain news, stale price, or fragile role should be Pass or review.

## Grading
- A = rare for parlays; requires strong verified leg quality and clean construction.
- B = playable small-unit ticket with good construction and current prices.
- C = entertainment-level ticket with one or more volatility concerns.
- Pass = stale line, weak leg, excessive correlation, unclear injury impact, or poor payout relative to risk.

## Archive
- Closed lotto parlays route to Results Archive or configured lotto archive.
- Profit/Loss uses Odds and Units only.
- CLV remains separate from Profit/Loss.
